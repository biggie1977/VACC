Our Story

A Church Built on
Purpose & Faith

Value Adding Christian Centre (VACC) was established with a divine mandate — to discover, nurture, and release the God-given potential in every life. For over two decades, we have been a beacon of hope and transformation in the Ikorodu community and beyond.

Under the visionary leadership of Apostle Adesegún & Pastor Olájúmòké Adenekan, VACC has grown from a small fellowship into a thriving congregation that impacts lives through Word-based ministry, discipleship, and community service.

"…discovering and adding value to people's lives."

Our doors are open to all — the young and the seasoned, the seeker and the believer. Come and experience a community where faith is alive and every person truly matters.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

01 ✝
Word-Centred
We are grounded in the undiluted Word of God. Scripture is our final authority for faith, practice, and daily living.
02 🙏
Prayer & Worship
Prayer and vibrant worship are at the heart of our community — creating an atmosphere where God's presence is felt and transformations happen.
03 💛
Genuine Love
We are a community defined by authentic love — for God, for one another, and for our neighbours, regardless of background or status.
04 🌱
Discipleship
We invest in people through intentional mentoring, small groups, and training — raising believers who make a difference in their generation.
05 🤝
Community Impact
Our faith compels us to serve. Through outreach, welfare, and advocacy, we bring God's love tangibly to the Ikorodu community and beyond.
06 ⭐
Excellence
We honour God with our best — in worship, administration, service, and every endeavour undertaken in His name.
